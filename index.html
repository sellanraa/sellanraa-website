who i am

My name is Connor Joseph Bell (b. 1979 in Oshkosh, WI USA). I currently live in the neighborhood of Beechmont in Louisville, KY USA with two little weens.

Music is a passion and I’ve publicly pursued sound in a variety of group outlets for over twenty-five years. At the same time, over the last twenty years, my solo vehicle has been Shedding. My other passion is history. I have a Master’s degree in history and taught for over twelve years before walking away in the fall of 2020. Perhaps I'll find a healthy atmosphere to return to education, as I do miss cultivating and engaging in meaningful, respectful, and thoughtful discussion. Until then, I’m exploring other paths.

Otherwise, I do a fair amount of the obvious (reading, cooking, gardening, travel), maybe the less obvious (watching baseball and hockey, playing tabletop roleplaying and strategy/war games), and finally I’m always trying to learn new things and keep up with the world’s events.

This website will gradually expand, but it’s currently the first little germinated seed from me dipping my toes into coding. If nothing else, it will help me think in different ways and I’m already making connections between traditional programming and applying it to computer music, composition, and synthesis.

    Sellanraa is the name of Isak’s farm in Knut Hamsun’s “Growth of the Soil”. The story of Isak and his farm have become something of a guidepost in my life.

    Of a patient, simple, and steady approach. Of accepting the uncontrollable. Of not being distracted by endless comparisons or thinking life is a race. Of embracing the process.
