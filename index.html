who i am

My name is Connor Joseph Bell (b. 1979 in Oshkosh, WI USA). I currently live in the neighborhood of Beechmont in Louisville, KY USA with my wife, Deidre, Harvey the cat, and three little weens. We love to spend time with the pets, tend our garden, and travel.

Music is a passion and I’ve publicly pursued sound for over twenty-five years. Over the last twenty years, my solo vehicle has been Shedding. My other passion is history. I have a Master’s degree in history and taught for over twelve years before walking away in the fall of 2020. I hope to find a healthy atmosphere to return to education, as I really miss cultivating and engaging in meaningful, respectful, and thoughtful discussion. Until then, I’m exploring career transition.

Otherwise, I do a fair amount of reading, watching baseball and hockey, gaming (tabletop roleplaying and strategy/war games) and I’m always trying to learn new things and keep up with the world’s events.

This website will gradually expand, but currently is the first little flower from a seed of me dipping my toes into coding. This process began with some Udemy courses on Python and Git and now I’m in the Code Louisville program. If nothing else, it will help me think in different ways and I’m already making connections between traditional programming and applying it to music with computer music (Max, Super Collider, and Tidal Cycles), composition, and synthesis.

    Sellanraa is the name of Isak’s farm in Knut Hamsun’s “Growth of the Soil”. The story of Isak and his farm have become something of a guidepost in my life.

    Of a patient, simple, and steady approach. Of accepting the uncontrollable. Of not being distracted by endless comparisons or thinking life is a race. Of embracing the process.
