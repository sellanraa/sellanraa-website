who i am

My name is Connor Joseph Bell (b. 1979 in Oshkosh, WI USA). I currently live in the beautiful neighborhood of Beechmont in Louisville, KY USA with my wife, Deidre, and three little weens. We love to spend time with the dogs, tend our garden, and travel. I’ve publicly pursued sound for over 20 years as Shedding as well as playing in a handful of bands over those years. Otherwise, I do a fair amount of reading, baseball and hockey watching, gaming (tabletop roleplaying and strategy/war games) and I’m always trying to learn new things and keep up with the world, which feels like a full-time job.

I have a Master’s degree in history and taught for over 12 years before walking away in the fall of 2020. I hope to find a healthy atmosphere to return to education, as I really miss cultivating and engaging in meaningful, respectful, and thoughtful discussion. Until then, I’m exploring other directions in life.

This website will gradually expand, but currently is the first little flower from a seed of me dipping my toes into coding. This process began with some Udemy courses on Python and Git and now I’m in the Code Louisville program. I’m hoping to continue to dabble. If nothing else, it will help me think in different ways and I’m already making connections between traditional programming and applying it to music with computer music (Max, Super Collider, and Tidal Cycles), composition, and synthesis.

    Sellanraa is the name of Isak’s farm in Knut Hamsun’s “Growth of the Soil”. The story of Isak and his farm have become something of a guidepost in my life.

    Of a patient, simple, and steady approach. Of accepting the uncontrollable. Of not being distracted by endless comparisons or thinking life is a race. Of embracing the process.
