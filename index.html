    • explained

    Sellanraa is the name of Isak’s farm in Knut Hamsun’s novel “Growth of the Soil”. The story of Isak and his farm have become something of a guidepost to strive for in my life:

    Of a patient, simple, and steady approach to life. Of accepting the uncontrollable. Of not being distracted by the endless comparisons in the competitive rat race of our reality. Of embracing the process.
