who and where i am

My name is Connor Bell (b. 1979 in Oshkosh, WI USA). Right now I’m living in Beechmont, a beautiful neighborhood in south Louisville with two wonderful little dachshunds that bring so much joy to my life. I’m a passionate person and spend my time pursuing knowledge and creativity. My curiosity feels insatiable and being creative, primarily through music, is an outlet for all that can’t be expressed so easily in life. After all, words are only one way to communicate.

I’ll keep an archive of these little essays linked with my writing samples. I didn’t really intend this to be a blog, and it’s certainly not a regular update, but I do like that I can trace a bit of a line by looking these over.

25.02.17: My contract with L3Harris ForceX Inc. via Insight Global ended on Valentine’s Day. I really appreciated my time there with a lot of great folks and a generally positive and energetic atmosphere. I think I put in some great work and I was proud when my manager said I was nominated for a quarterly performance award. Of course, being a contractor means I was ineligible, but it was still nice to feel like I was appreciated. Theoretically, a position is being opened for a technical writer position there, but last year was a lesson that nothing can be taken for granted so I will be continuing the hunt for the new normal.

As sad as I am to be leaving the workplace, I’m probably more sad that I’ll no longer be spending time with family in Nashville. To live in a relatively healthy family environment and to absorb the hustle and bustle energy of an active family was meaningful. It was a window into another way of living that isn’t my norm and I’ll forever be grateful to them for opening their home to me and creating such a special memory that I’ll hold onto..

So with all that said: I’m back in the soup. I appreciate any eyes and ears out there on my behalf. In the meantime, I’ll continue to work to be the best me I can be.
