who and where i am

My name is Connor Joseph Bell (b. 1979 in Oshkosh, WI USA). Right now I’m living in Beechmont, a beautiful neighborhood in south Louisville with two wonderful little dachshunds that bring so much joy to my life. I’m a passionate person and spend my time pursuing knowledge and creativity. My curiosity feels insatiable and being creative, primarily through music, is an outlet for all that can’t be expressed so easily in life. After all, words are only one way to express oneself.

23.08.15:

I’m 44 today. Life is strange and complicated, but I’m so incredibly grateful for where I am and how I got here. The rough waters have abated and I’m so excited to see where the ride takes me. I love living my simple and quiet life. Music has been going well with the band and I’m trying to gear up to do a handful of shows on my own this fall in the upper midwest. I’m hoping to announce those soon enough on the Shedding page. Maybe from there I’ll investigate some other regional extended weekend possibilities in the winter or spring. That said, it’s hard to leave the weens for too long and I really need some outdoor adventure more than playing shows. Finally, I feel like I’m at a point with my new job ~9 months in where things are starting to come together more and more often and there is a foundation that I can build on. It’s amazing how stressful it has been to completely shift careers and, while I dearly miss teaching, I’m thrilled to explore this new direction and learn so many new things.

    Sellanraa is the name of Isak’s farm in Knut Hamsun’s “Growth of the Soil”. The story of Isak and his farm have become something of a guidepost in my life.

    Of a patient, simple, and steady approach. Of accepting the uncontrollable. Of not being distracted by endless comparisons or thinking life is a race. Of embracing the process.
