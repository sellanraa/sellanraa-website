sellanraa

me the weens shedding

explained

Sellanraa is the name of Isak’s farm in Knut Hamsun’s novel “Growth of the Soil”. The story of Isak and his farm have become something of a guidepost in my life to strive for:

Of a patient, simple, and steady approach to life. Of accepting the uncontrollable. Of not being distracted by the endless comparisons in the competitive rat race of our reality. Of embracing the process.

See also: Chop wood, carry water. Wabi-sabi.
