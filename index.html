who and where i am

My name is Connor Bell (b. 1979 in Oshkosh, WI USA). Right now I’m living in Beechmont, a beautiful neighborhood in south Louisville with two wonderful little dachshunds that bring so much joy to my life. I’m a passionate person and spend my time pursuing knowledge and creativity. My curiosity feels insatiable and being creative, primarily through music, is an outlet for all that can’t be expressed so easily in life. After all, words are only one way to express oneself.

I’ll keep an archive of these little essays linked with my writing samples. I didn’t really intend this to be a blog, and it’s certainly not a regular update, but I do like that I can trace a bit of a line by looking these over.

24.07.15

I just lost two months of my life waiting for a job that never came together despite all assurances from everyone involved. I was emailed by the recruiter saying that the company hadn’t completed necessary paperwork less than an hour before the time I was supposed to be there on my first day. A week and a half later, after ten weeks since the initial offer, the project was canceled and the job I was so excited about evaporated before I could even start. The lesson one friend shared was to always be interviewing. It’s such a different paradigm than the stability of education. I drafted a twelve page document detailing every step just to organize my thoughts. Someday, maybe I’ll share it.

I’m so ready to get back to work, but then, that was true the day I was laid off. I have a great support system and enough of a cushion not to fully freak out. Still, I just want to continue to grow professionally and contribute to something larger than myself. All in all, life is good though and I’m thankful.
