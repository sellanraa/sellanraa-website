    AKA Sweet Pea, Spider-Ween, Little Monkey, Little Billy Goat, Pup, Little Shadow, Soul-Sniffer, Bee-Killer, Berry-Eater.

    Dagny was a sweet little malnourished puppymill case. She was supposed to be skittish so I planned to name her Freydis, so I could call her Freydi. But I saw no fear, so I named her Dagny, which has Norwegian roots meaning 'new day'. I decided a young pup would be a welcome break from the emotional and intense care of a senior and in a few years, Ruby will be there and, in the meantime, she can teach Dagny the ways of Clan Murph.
