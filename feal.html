the free electronic association of louisville

about

    FEAL serves to further free electronic sound exchange in Louisville, Kentucky, primarily through live performance and recorded exchange.

guiding principles

    Wherefore, the Association is to supersede any individual and is not to fracture from infighting borne of interpretational conflicts or Maoist political philosophy as has led to the dismemberment of improvisational predecessors.
    Association members to be Louisville residents, paying no membership dues and directing any proceeds borne of the Association towards Association endeavors and expenses.
    Association performances to include a plural number of members, to be comprised only of members, and to explore pure synthesis only. Other sonic exploration to have no place in the Association.
    Association performances to be documented. Selected performances to be archived and made available.
    These guiding principles are subject to amendment and shall be interpreted as living principles, adaptable to the evolving needs of the Association.
