    AKA Peanut. The Persnickety Peanut. Stinky Tinky. The Boop Queen. The Midnight Pooper.

    Her real name is Tinker Bell, but that is unacceptable. Tink is our most recent foster (seventh and last for now) and we wanted a break from the chaos for a bit, and since she was a bit of a tougher sell (old and leaky), we figured we’d give her a good home. She's a mini and was immediately comfortable because she just sort of exists. She's also unafraid to boop you until you give her whatever she wants. We think she’s around 13 and maybe has some physiological or developmental issues that mess with her balance and coordination. She doesn't really 'do' walks, but she has endless energy when chasing a ball in the backyard.
