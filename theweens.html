    My ex-wife and I fostered weens through the Kentucky Dachshund Rescue, on and off, for the past several years. They are my muse.

    Many pictures are taken with my cell phone, a Google Pixel, since the best camera is the one you have with you. I also use a Sony A6000 camera with Sony 18-105mm f/4 and 35mm f/1.8 prime lenses.

    Pictures on each page above are posted in chronological order.
