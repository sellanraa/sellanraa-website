explained

My wife and I have fostered weens through the Kentucky Dachshund Rescue, on and off, for the past several years. They are my muse.

Many pictures are taken with my cell phone, a Google Pixel 3 (currently), since the best camera is the one you have with you. I also use a Sony A6000 camera with Sony 18-105mm f/4 and 35mm f/1.8 prime lenses.
